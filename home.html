Welcome to Suprativa

Established in 1993, Suprativa is a non-profit, volunteer-driven organization committed to improving the lives of the underprivileged in Odisha, India. The organization concentrates on essential areas including health, education, environmental sustainability, livelihood enhancement, road safety, science communication, disaster preparedness, community media, and emergency relief. Suprativa aims to uplift and empower marginalized communities through its dedicated efforts.

Our mission is to bring hope, aspiration, and joy to everyone by unlocking the inner talent and potential (suprativa) within each individual, enabling them to perform their best. We envision a society brimming with peace, joy, happiness, and harmony, where every person enjoys freedom from poverty, ignorance, hunger, fear, and exploitation. Our goal is to affirm basic human values, humanity, and mutual respect in all our endeavors.

Our key strength lies in the extensive knowledge and familiarity of our professionals in the field of social work, coupled with the unwavering support and trust of the communities we serve. As a grassroots-level organization, Suprativa is deeply rooted in the society it aims to uplift, ensuring that our efforts are both relevant and impactful.

By focusing on sustainable development goals and leveraging the innate potential of individuals, Suprativa is committed to creating a better, more equitable world for all.
